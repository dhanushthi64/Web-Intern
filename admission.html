ADMISSIONS

Every year, admissions at Sunbeam are open to all classes: LKG to 9th Grade and 11th Grade. All vacant seats are filled on a first-come-first-serve basis. Admissions to 10th and 12th Grades are accepted in special situations, for example, a change of location of the family owing to the job transfer of parents. The Admissions office can be contacted for the same.
